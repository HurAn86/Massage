About our Massage

Our Massage has combined the techniques of our massage and eastern massage to give you the most rejuvenating massage that will help your body, mind and spirit to revive. To help maintain your good health, we recommend clients to have regular massages.
Our team operated the New School for Massage for nearly 10 years. The owner has been in the massage therapy field for nearly 30 years. We understand that a good massage treatment involves hospitality, service, and massage skills that are grounded in science but delivered as art.
